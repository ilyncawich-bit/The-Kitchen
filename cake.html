Authentic Tres Leches Cake

A light, airy sponge cake soaked in a rich mixture of three milks—transforming into something impossibly moist and tender.

Cake Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 5 large eggs (separated)
  • 1 cup granulated sugar (divided)
  • 1/3 cup whole milk & 1 tsp vanilla extract

The Three Milks

  • 12 oz evaporated milk
  • 14 oz sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/4 cup whole milk

Detailed Instructions

  1. Prep: Preheat oven to 350°F. Spray a 9x13 inch pan.
  2. Base: Whisk flour, baking powder, and salt. Beat egg yolks with 3/4 cup sugar until pale. Stir in milk and vanilla, then combine with dry ingredients.
  3. Whip: Beat egg whites to soft peaks. Add remaining 1/4 cup sugar and beat to stiff peaks. Gently fold into batter.
  4. Bake: Pour into pan and bake 25-35 minutes. Cool completely.
  5. Soak: Whisk the 3 milks together. Poke holes all over the cooled cake with a fork and slowly pour the milk mixture over the top.
  6. Chill: Refrigerate at least 4 hours (or overnight). Top with whipped cream and cinnamon before serving.
