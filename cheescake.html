Oreo Dream Cheesecake

A silky cream cheese base speckled with chunks of dark cocoa cookies on a crunchy Oreo crust.

Ingredients

  • 24 Oreo cookies (crushed for crust)
  • 4 tbsp Melted butter
  • 24 oz Softened cream cheese
  • 1 cup Sugar
  • 12 Oreos (chopped for filling)

Instructions

  1. Press Oreo crumbs and butter into a pan; bake 10 mins at 325Â°F.
  2. Beat cream cheese and sugar; fold in chopped Oreos.
  3. Pour into crust and bake 55 mins.
  4. Chill for 8 hours before serving.
