Rustic Classic Lasagna

A towering masterpiece of slow-cooked meat ragu, herb-infused ricotta, and sheets of golden pasta.

Ingredients

  • 12 Lasagna noodles
  • 1 lb Ground beef
  • 15 oz Ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups Shredded Mozzarella
  • 24 oz Marinara sauce

Instructions

  1. Brown beef and mix with marinara sauce.
  2. In a dish, layer sauce, noodles, ricotta, and mozzarella. Repeat 3 times.
  3. Cover with foil and bake at 375Â°F for 25 mins.
  4. Uncover and bake 15 mins more until bubbling.
