Classic Cuervo® Margarita

A sophisticated blend of tart citrus and earthy agave, creating the ultimate refreshing escape in a glass.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz Cuervo Tradicional® Blanco Tequila
  • 1 oz Freshly squeezed lime juice
  • 1 oz Triple Sec or Cointreau
  • 1/2 oz Agave nectar
  • Coarse sea salt for the rim

Instructions

  1. Rub a lime wedge around the rim of your glass and dip in salt.
  2. Combine tequila, lime juice, liqueur, and agave in a shaker with ice.
  3. Shake vigorously for 15 seconds until the shaker is frosted.
  4. Strain into the prepared glass over fresh ice and garnish with a lime wheel.
