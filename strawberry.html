Strawberry Colada

A luscious, frozen fusion of sun-ripened strawberries and creamy Caribbean coconut milk.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz White Rum
  • 1 1/2 cups Frozen strawberries
  • 3 oz Cream of coconut
  • 2 oz Pineapple juice

Instructions

  1. Place all ingredients into a high-speed blender.
  2. Add a handful of ice and blend until completely smooth.
  3. Pour into a tall chilled glass and garnish with a fresh strawberry.
